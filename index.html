    About Pomodoro Timer

    The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method developed by Francesco Cirillo in the late 1980s. It uses a timer to break work into intervals, traditionally 25 minutes in length, separated by short breaks.

    After four pomodoros, you take a longer break of 15-30 minutes. This technique helps to improve focus and productivity by creating a sense of urgency and allowing for regular breaks.

    Try it out and see how it can help you manage your time better!
